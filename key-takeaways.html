Core Principles

1% Better Every Day

Small improvements compound over time. Getting just 1% better daily leads to 37x improvement after a year.

The Habit Loop

All habits follow this cycle:

  • Cue → Craving → Response → Reward

The 4 Laws of Behavior Change

1. Make It Obvious

  • Use implementation intentions
  • Design your environment for visibility

2. Make It Attractive

  • Use temptation bundling
  • Join cultures where your habit is normal

3. Make It Easy

  • Reduce friction
  • Use the 2-minute rule

4. Make It Satisfying

  • Use immediate rewards
  • Track your habits
