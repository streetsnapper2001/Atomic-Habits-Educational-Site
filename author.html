About the Author

James Clear is a bestselling author and internationally renowned speaker best known for his book, Atomic Habits. He specializes in the topics of behavior change, decision-making, and continuous improvement. His writing draws from scientific research and practical insights, helping millions of readers worldwide build better habits and systems for long-term success.

James Clear's work has influenced leaders in business, sports, and healthcare. He is a regular speaker at Fortune 500 companies, and his ideas have been featured in Time magazine, The New York Times, and on CBS This Morning. His newsletter reaches over a million subscribers weekly, and his philosophy centers on the belief that small changes lead to big results.

James Clear is one of the world's leading experts on habit formation and behavioral psychology.

His bestselling book Atomic Habits has sold over 15 million copies worldwide and has been translated into more than 50 languages. The book has spent over 200 weeks on the New York Times bestseller list and continues to help millions of people build better habits and break bad ones.

Clear's work focuses on the intersection of psychology, neuroscience, and human performance. He believes that small changes compound into remarkable results, and his evidence-based approach has helped individuals, teams, and organizations improve their performance.

Before becoming a full-time writer and speaker, Clear was a successful entrepreneur who grew his blog from zero to 500,000+ email subscribers. His insights on productivity, habits, and decision-making are sought after by Fortune 500 companies, professional sports teams, and educational institutions worldwide.

He regularly speaks at conferences and corporate events, sharing practical strategies for building better systems and achieving long-term success through small, consistent improvements.
